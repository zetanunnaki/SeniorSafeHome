## How to choose

1. **Check your tub surface** — suction mats need a smooth, non-porous floor. Textured tubs need a teak or lay-flat mat.
2. **Prioritize drainage** — more holes means faster drying and less mold.
3. **Match the size** to your tub; extra-long mats give fuller coverage.
4. **Replace mats** when the suction weakens.
