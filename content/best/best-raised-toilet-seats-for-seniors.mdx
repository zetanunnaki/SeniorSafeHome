## How to choose

1. **Bolt-on vs. freestanding.** Bolt-on locking seats are the most stable; freestanding frames are best when you can't modify the toilet.
2. **Match your bowl shape** — confirm standard (round) or elongated fit.
3. **Add support arms** for hip and knee strain, or pair a no-arm seat with a toilet-side grab bar.
4. **Check weight capacity** and seat width for comfort.
