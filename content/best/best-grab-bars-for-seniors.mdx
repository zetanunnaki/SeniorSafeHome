## How to choose a grab bar

1. **Match length to the spot.** 24" for shower entries and long spans; 16–18" beside the toilet; 12" for tight corners.
2. **Anchor into studs.** A 500 lb rating only holds with solid backing. Use a stud finder, or have a handyman install — it's quick and inexpensive.
3. **Prioritize grip texture** where hands get wet (shower entry).
4. **Add bars in pairs** — one at the tub entry and one inside, plus one by the toilet, covers the riskiest moments.
