## How to choose

1. **Match the dosing schedule** — once-daily, AM/PM, or 3x–4x daily.
2. **Prioritize easy-open lids** for arthritis or weak grip.
3. **Look for bold, permanent labels** and large compartments.
4. **Consider detachable daily pods** if the user goes out during the day.
