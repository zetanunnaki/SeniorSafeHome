## Which cushion type fits the need?

- **Coccyx (U-cutout) memory foam** — everyday tailbone and lower-back pain.
- **Donut** — hemorrhoids, postpartum, and post-surgery recovery.
- **Honeycomb gel** — cooling and pressure dispersion for hot, long sitting.
- **Ventilated / medical-grade wheelchair** — all-day wheelchair use and pressure-sore prevention.

## How to choose

1. **Match the shape to the problem** — coccyx cutout for tailbone, donut for sensitive recovery.
2. **Foam vs. gel** — foam for molded support and warmth, gel for cooling and pressure dispersion.
3. **For wheelchairs**, choose a ventilated, non-slip, pressure-relief design.
4. **Mind the cover** — washable, breathable, and (for incontinence) waterproof.
