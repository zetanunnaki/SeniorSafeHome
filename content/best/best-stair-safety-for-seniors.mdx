## What do your stairs need?

- **Indoor wooden stairs** — soft carpet treads for grip, warmth, and quieter steps.
- **Outdoor steps (porch, deck, concrete)** — all-weather rubber treads that shrug off rain and snow.
- **1–5 entry steps** — an easy-install wall-mount or adjustable handrail for a solid handhold.
- **Open staircase with no wall** — a freestanding railing kit.

## How to choose

1. **Match the tread to the surface** — carpet for indoor wood/tile, rubber for outdoor/wet steps.
2. **Cover the full run** — measure your stairs and buy enough treads for every step.
3. **Add a handrail** — on both sides if possible; check weight rating and secure mounting.
4. **Mind the fit** — many treads are cuttable; handrails like the Metty adjust to fit 1–5 steps.
