## Which type do you need?

- **Reusable bed pads** — catch overnight accidents on top of the sheet; washable hundreds of times.
- **Mattress protectors** — go under the sheet to shield the whole mattress from moisture (and bed bugs).
- **Chair pads** — seat-sized, non-slip protection for wheelchairs, recliners, and car seats.
- **Positioning pads** — handled pads that also help caregivers turn and lift safely.

## How to choose

1. **Layer protector + pad** — mattress protector under the sheet, washable pad on top.
2. **Match absorbency to need** — 6–8 cups for heavy overnight incontinence.
3. **Check the backing** — a non-slip bottom keeps pads from sliding on the sheet.
4. **Mind comfort** — quiet, soft, breathable surfaces matter for sleep and skin.
