## How to choose

1. **Prioritize a full-hand trigger** for arthritis or weak grip.
2. **Match the length** — 32" for everyday, longer for high shelves, short for seated use.
3. **Add a dressing stick and sock aid** after hip or knee surgery.
4. **Look for a magnetic tip** to pick up keys and coins.
