## How to choose

1. **Measure your tub** width and wall height — the bench must straddle the wall with stable footing inside and out.
2. **Check the feet** — suction inside the tub plus non-slip rubber outside is the most secure combination.
3. **Reversible backrest** lets you set entry from either side.
4. **Consider a sliding seat** for caregiver assistance or very limited mobility.
