## How to choose

1. **Decide tray vs. basket** — flat surface for meals, basket for contained carrying.
2. **Check the fit** — standard walker width, rollator front bar, or universal clamp.
3. **Prefer secure attachment** — buckles and balanced loading prevent tipping.
4. **Add a cup holder** for hydration on any mobility aid.
