## How to choose

1. **Match amplification to the hearing loss** — 26dB for moderate, 50–65dB for severe.
2. **Corded for outage-proof reliability; cordless for whole-home use.**
3. **Photo speed-dial** helps seniors with memory or vision changes.
4. **Confirm hearing-aid compatibility** if the user wears hearing aids.
