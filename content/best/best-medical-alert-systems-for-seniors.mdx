## How to choose

1. **Decide monitored vs. no-fee** — a 24/7 center dispatches help; a pager alerts a caregiver in the home.
2. **In-home vs. mobile** based on how often the senior goes out.
3. **Add fall detection** for higher-risk users.
4. **Pick a wearable they'll actually wear** every day, including in the shower where many falls happen.
