## How much support do you need?

- **Pivoting / single-tip canes** (HurryCane, HONEYBULL, REHAND, Vive) — light, foldable, great for mild balance support and everyday use. Pivoting bases adjust on uneven ground and stand on their own.
- **Quad (4-point) canes** (KINGGEAR, Medline) — maximum stability; physician-recommended for post-stroke, post-surgery, or significant balance issues.
- **Bariatric canes** (NOVA, Sky Med) — reinforced for 500–600 lb.

## How to choose

1. **Match support to need** — single-tip for mild balance, quad for serious stability.
2. **Get the height right** — handle at the wrist crease when standing tall.
3. **Pick the handle** — offset handles are easiest on an arthritic wrist.
4. **Check capacity** — bariatric canes reach 500–600 lb; tall users need extended-length models.

<Callout type="safety" title="Replace worn tips">
A cane is only as safe as its rubber tip. Check it regularly and replace it the moment the tread wears smooth — a worn tip can slip on hard floors.
</Callout>
