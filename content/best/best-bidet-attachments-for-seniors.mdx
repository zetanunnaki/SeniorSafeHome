## Which bidet is right?

- **Non-electric attachment** — installs between seat and bowl; single or dual nozzle; cheapest and renter-friendly.
- **Handheld sprayer** — seated, one-hand directional control; best for arthritis and limited mobility.
- **Electric seat** — heated seat, warm water, warm-air dryer; the most hands-free, can replace toilet paper.

## How to choose

1. **Pick the type** — attachment for simplicity, handheld for control, electric for hands-free.
2. **Single or dual nozzle** — dual adds a frontal feminine wash.
3. **Cold vs. warm water** — electric seats give warm water; attachments are usually cold.
4. **Check the toilet fit** — slim models fit tight seat gaps; most fit standard toilets.
