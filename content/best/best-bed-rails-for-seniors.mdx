## How to choose

1. **Confirm bed type** — home bed vs. adjustable frame — and pick a compatible rail.
2. **Assist rail vs. full-length** depending on whether you need a handhold or roll-out protection.
3. **Check anchoring** — under-mattress bars plus tie-down straps are the most secure.
4. **Verify ASTM F3186-17 certification** and a no-gap fit.
