- **Early wandering** — a simple, loud door/window alarm on key exits (GE/UltraPro).

- **Memory care / group homes** — a professional exit stopper with key override (STI-6400).
- **Calling for help** — pair with a caregiver call button so your loved one can summon you.

## How to choose

1. **Match the alarm to the stage** — simple alarm early; pager or motion sensor as wandering grows.
2. **Check the range** — make sure the receiver reaches wherever you'll be in the home.
3. **Make it loud enough** — 100 dB+ with a visual LED helps caregivers with hearing loss.
4. **Layer sensors** — door + motion gives the most dependable warning.
