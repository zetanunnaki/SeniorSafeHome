Safety equipment doesn't need to be expensive to be good. The Amazon Basics 24-inch grab bar delivers the same core safety as premium bars — genuine 500 lb weight capacity — at a value price, which makes it the smart pick when you're outfitting a whole bathroom.

## Why it works for seniors

This is a true weight-bearing bar: brushed stainless steel, rated to 500 lb when anchored into studs, with concealed mounting screws for a tidy look. It has thousands of strong reviews, and because it's inexpensive, you can install several — at the shower entry, inside the shower, and beside the toilet — without breaking the budget.
