If a senior bathes in a standard bathtub and finds stepping over the wall risky, a transfer bench is the answer — and the Medline is our top pick. It solves the core problem securely, with footing engineered for both surfaces it stands on.

## Why it works for seniors

The senior sits on the outer half and slides across, never lifting a leg over the slick tub wall. What sets the Medline apart is its **dual-grip footing**: suction cups inside the tub, slip-resistant rubber outside. Push-button height adjustment and a reversible backrest make it easy to set up in any bathroom, and the aluminum frame resists rust.
