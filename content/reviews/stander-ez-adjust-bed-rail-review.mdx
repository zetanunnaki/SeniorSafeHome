When a senior needs more than a handhold — when rolling out of bed is a real risk — a full-length rail is the answer. The Stander EZ Adjust is our pick: it's a long, adjustable barrier that still gets out of the way when you need to change the sheets.

## Why it works for seniors

The rail adjusts from 26 to 42 inches, so you can size the barrier to the person and bed. It gives a solid handhold for the sit-to-stand transfer and a longer guard that helps prevent roll-outs during sleep. A safety strap secures it to the bed frame, and it folds flat 180° for bedding changes. An organizer pouch keeps a phone and glasses within reach.
