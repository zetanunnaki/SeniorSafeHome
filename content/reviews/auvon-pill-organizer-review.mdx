The hardest part of using a pill box, for many seniors, is simply getting the lid open. The AUVON spring-open organizer solves that elegantly: the lids pop open by themselves. It's one of the most arthritis-friendly designs available, and it costs only a few dollars.

## Why it works for seniors

Press the button and a spring pops the lid open — no prying, no pinching, no strain on stiff or weak hands. The compartments are extra-large (they hold around eight fish-oil capsules), the days are color-coded for easy reading, and it's inexpensive enough to buy a couple.
