With tens of thousands of reviews, the Drive Medical Tub Transfer Bench is the most popular transfer bench on the market — and after using it, it's easy to see why. It does the one thing that matters most exceptionally well: it removes the need to step over a slick, high tub wall.

## Why it works for seniors

The bench straddles the tub wall — two legs inside, two outside — so a senior sits on the outer half and slides across, staying seated and supported the whole time. The A-frame is sturdy, the seat has drainage holes, and the height adjusts in half-inch increments to match the user. It's reversible, so it works no matter which side of the tub you enter from.
