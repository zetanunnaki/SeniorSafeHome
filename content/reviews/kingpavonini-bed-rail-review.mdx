Getting in and out of bed is one of the riskiest moments of a senior's day. A bed assist rail gives them something solid to grip — and the KingPavonini is our value pick because it's certified, stable, and simple to set up.

## Why it works for seniors

The L-shaped rail anchors under the mattress and offers an ergonomic curved handle to grip while sitting up and standing. It's certified to ASTM F3186-17, rated to 380 lb, fits beds from Twin to King, and includes a handy storage pocket for a phone or glasses. Setup is tool-free.
