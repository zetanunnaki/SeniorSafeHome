A large-button phone keeps a senior connected and able to call for help, and the AT&T CD4930 is the everyday-value pick that gets the basics right: big buttons, a readable display, and adjustable sound — at a fair price.

## Why it works for seniors

The buttons are large and high-contrast, and the backlit display tilts up so it's easy to read from a chair. An audio-assist boost and four preset equalizer profiles let you tune the sound to the user's hearing, and it's hearing-aid compatible. Because it's corded, it never needs charging and keeps working in a power outage — a real advantage in an emergency. A built-in answering machine rounds it out.
