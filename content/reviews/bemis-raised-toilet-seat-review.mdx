Most raised toilet seats clamp on and can shift or wobble over time. The Bemis Snap 2 Secure is different: it bolts directly to the toilet like a regular seat, so it never moves — which is exactly what you want when a senior is lowering and lifting their full weight.

## Why it works for seniors

The bolt-on Snap 2 Secure system locks the seat to the bowl with zero wobble, and the unit is rated to a remarkable 1,000 lb. Ergonomic aluminum arms give a stable push-up surface for the sit-to-stand motion that strains hips and knees, and a Clean Shield guard keeps things hygienic. It's occupational-therapist recommended and made in the USA.

The Bemis Snap 2 Secure costs more than a basic clamp-on seat, but it buys genuine peace of mind: it bolts on, never wobbles, and holds far more weight than anyone will put on it. For maximum security, it's the one we'd choose.
