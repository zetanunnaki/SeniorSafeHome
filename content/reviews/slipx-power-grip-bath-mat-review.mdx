When grip security is the priority — for a senior with significant balance concerns, say — the SlipX Solutions Power Grip is the mat to get. Its oversized suction cups deliver the firmest hold we tested.

## Why it works for seniors

The underside packs **365 suction cups that are larger and stronger** than most competitors', so the mat locks onto a smooth tub floor and stays put. At 39 inches it covers more of the tub than standard mats, extending secure footing, and the soft-touch vinyl surface is comfortable underfoot. Hexagonal drain holes let water escape.
