If we could recommend only one grab bar, it would be the Moen Home Care. It nails the fundamentals of a safety product: real strength, secure mounting, and a finish that holds up in a wet bathroom for years.

## Why it works for seniors

This is a true weight-bearing bar — rated to 500 lb when anchored into studs — so it can actually catch a fall, not just steady a wobble. The concealed mounting screws give it a clean, almost towel-bar look that blends into the bathroom, and the stainless steel shrugs off moisture and corrosion. Moen backs it with a lifetime warranty.
