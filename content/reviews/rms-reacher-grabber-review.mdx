A good reacher keeps a senior independent without bending, stretching, or climbing — three common causes of falls. The RMS 32-inch grabber is our top pick because it nails the one feature that matters most for older hands: an easy, full-hand trigger.

## Why it works for seniors

Instead of a single-finger trigger that strains arthritic hands, the RMS uses a full-hand squeeze that spreads the effort across the whole palm. The rubberized jaw rotates to grab items at any angle, the aluminum frame is light to hold, and because it comes as a two-pack, you can keep one upstairs and one down — or by the bed and by the chair.
