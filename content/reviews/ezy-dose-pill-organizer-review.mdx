Medication mistakes are a common, preventable hazard for seniors, and a clear, easy-to-open organizer is the simplest fix. The Ezy Dose push-button box is our top pick because it solves the problem older hands struggle with most: getting the lid open.

## Why it works for seniors

The push-button lids pop open with one hand and almost no grip strength — the reason it earned an Arthritis Foundation Ease-of-Use commendation. The compartments are extra-large (they hold big fish-oil capsules), the day labels are bold and easy to read, and at a couple of dollars it's effortless to replace or buy several.

## Who it's best for

<Callout type="tip" title="Best for">

</Callout>

<Callout type="info" title="Not ideal for">

## The verdict

For most seniors, the Ezy Dose is the right pill organizer: it's the easiest to open, the compartments are roomy, and it costs almost nothing. If the routine is more complex, step up to a multi-dose model — but for simple regimens, this is the one.
