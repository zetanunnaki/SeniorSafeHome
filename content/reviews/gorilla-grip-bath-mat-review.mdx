If you buy one thing to make a bathroom safer, make it a non-slip mat — and the Gorilla Grip is the one we recommend. With well over 100,000 reviews and a price under most restaurant lunches, it's the highest-value safety upgrade in the house.

## Why it works for seniors

The underside is covered in hundreds of suction cups that lock onto a smooth tub floor, turning a slick surface into a stable one. Drainage holes let water escape so it doesn't pool, and the whole mat goes in the washing machine when it needs cleaning. For a senior, that means confident footing exactly where falls most often happen.
